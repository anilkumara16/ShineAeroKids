Welcome to Shine Aerokids International Pre school

At Shine Aerokids, every child embarks on a journey of discovery and growth. Our vibrant, nurturing environment is designed to inspire curiosity and foster a love for learning from an early age.

We blend creative play with foundational education, guided by dedicated educators who are committed to each child's development. Join us in shaping bright futures and unlocking the potential within every child.

Aerokids Programs

Explore our diverse range of programs designed to foster early learning and development, each tailored to ignite curiosity and support growth in a fun, engaging environment.

Play Group

Age Group: 2 to 3 YEARS

Discover our Play Group programs, designed to provide a nurturing environment where children aged 2-3 can explore and learn through play. Our programs offer a blend of creative activities, structured play, and social interactions, helping to build foundational skills in communication, cooperation, and problem-solving. Through engaging games, arts and crafts, and sensory experiences, children develop confidence, social skills, and a love for learning in a supportive and fun atmosphere.

Pre-KG

Age Group: 3 to 4 YEARS

Join our Pre-KG programs, where children aged 3-4 embark on an enriching educational journey that combines structured learning with playful exploration. Our curriculum focuses on developing early literacy, numeracy, and social skills through interactive activities, hands-on projects, and collaborative play, preparing young learners for a successful transition to kindergarten.

Junior-KG

Age Group: 4 to 5 YEARS

Discover our Junior-KG programs for children aged 4-5, offering an exciting blend of learning and play. Our engaging curriculum focuses on advancing essential skills, nurturing creativity, and encouraging social interaction, all while preparing your child for a successful start in primary school. Through interactive lessons, hands-on activities, and group projects, children build a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy, develop problem-solving abilities, and grow their confidence. Our experienced educators provide a supportive and stimulating environment where every child can thrive and embrace their potential.

Senior-KG

Age Group: 5 to 6 YEARS

Explore our Senior-KG programs, where children aged 5-6 engage in a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare them for primary school. Our program emphasizes advanced literacy and numeracy skills, critical thinking, and social development through interactive activities, collaborative projects, and creative expression, ensuring each child is well-equipped for their educational journey ahead.
